How We Predict Your JEE Mains Rank from Percentile

The Basic Formula

We use a simple formula to estimate your rank based on your percentile:

Rank = ((100 - Percentile) / 100) * Total_Candidates + 1

Total Candidates

We assume a total of 1,450,000 candidates based on previous year's data.

Category-Specific Calculations

Depending on your category, we adjust the calculation:

  • General: We only calculate the overall rank
  • OBC: We calculate both overall rank and category rank (using 550,362 candidates)
  • SC: We calculate both overall rank and category rank (using 132,167 candidates)
  • ST: We calculate both overall rank and category rank (using 46,805 candidates)
  • EWS: We calculate both overall rank and category rank (using 149,767 candidates)

Rounding

The final rank is rounded to the nearest whole number.

Important Notes

  • This is an estimation based on previous data and may not be 100% accurate.
  • The actual number of candidates may vary each year.
  • For very high percentiles, the prediction may be less precise due to the density of candidates at the top.
